"More Than Spaghetti..."

It's time to ditch that jar of pasta sauce and start cooking something easy and ten times more tasty!

Our Favourite's

Tomato Pasta Sauce

Ok so I know we promised More Than Spaghetti but this tomato sauce is an essential. Quick, healthy, easy, and super tasty. It can be served with pasta or with rice and a piece of meat or fish.
Servings: 4

Chicken fajitas

A delicious spicy dish that only needs one pot! You can get inventive with this dish and throw in any leftovers that are lurking in the back of the fridge: peppers, mushrooms, courgettes...
Servings: 3

Risotto

Another one pot dish, with minimal ingredients but great taste.
This authentic italian dish is brilliant if you're looking for something cheap.
It's not great on speed but it is great on taste.
Servings: 2

Chilli con Carne

You can make this dish as spicy as you like! To give it a real Mexican twist,
add a side of nachos and dig in!
Servings: 4
